Giving Back

Engineering for good — supporting nonprofits and mission-driven organizations.

"Better to do a Good Deed Near at Home than go far away to burn incense."
— Amelia Earhart

I believe engineering can positively impact lives and help build social movements. The skills I've developed across aerospace, software, and public health aren't just tools for a career — they're ways to solve real problems for people who need it most.

If you're a nonprofit or socially-conscious group that needs web development or software services, I'd love to hear from you. I'm open to taking on projects at reduced rates or pro bono, based on your organization's needs and my availability.

Whether you need a website built from scratch, an existing system improved, or help thinking through a technical problem — reach out. Good engineering shouldn't be out of reach for organizations doing good work.
